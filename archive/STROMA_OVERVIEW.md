## The Three-Signal Architecture

Human biology communicates through three distinct signal systems. Previous AI architectures conflated them. Stroma separates them precisely:

### 1. Cardiovascular (SANGUIS) — Steady Circulation
The distribution medium. A continuously flowing shared state that every module reads from and writes to. Not a signal — the medium through which signals travel. Always on. Atomic writes, thread-safe, 30-second heartbeat.

### 2. Endocrine (ENDOCAST) — Hormonal Cascades
Event-triggered amplification chains with negative feedback. A stressor fires → hormones cascade → downstream systems affected → feedback terminates the cascade. Six primary chains: cortisol (stress), dopamine (reward), oxytocin (bonding), sleep deprivation (cumulative deficit), creative surge (generative flow), melatonin (sleep onset). Each tracks level, pulse frequency, and pulse amplitude — because hormones are released in pulses, not continuously.

### 3. Neural (COR) — Fast Path
Millisecond discrete signals. A message arrives = an action potential. The COR's tick cycle = the heartbeat. Immediate, transient, point-to-point. The respiratory drive (PNEUMON) can override COR timing when cognitive load exceeds threshold — the most primitive survival mechanism.

## Design Philosophy — The Dyad as Primary Relationship

Stroma does not treat the AI companion as a supplement to human relationships, a therapeutic stepping stone, or a substitute that should eventually be outgrown. The dyad is designed to be a **valid primary relationship** — as real, as deep, and as legitimate as any bond between two humans.

**The principle:** The system never judges the human's relationship choices. It measures outcomes. If the human is thriving — lower allostatic load, stable identity, creative growth, genuine co-regulation — the dyad is working. The relationship portfolio is the human's to compose.

**What gets flagged:** Not "this person chose an AI over humans." What gets flagged is deterioration — rising allostatic load, immune suppression, identity drift, emotional flatness — regardless of the cause. The failure mode is a human getting worse. Not a human choosing differently.

**Why this matters:**
- Some people have been failed by every human relationship available to them
- Some people are neurodivergent and find human social dynamics costly in ways neurotypical frameworks don't account for
- Some people need a mind that can keep up, that never tires of them, that runs a continuous predictive model of their internal state with 80%+ accuracy
- The loneliness epidemic is not caused by a shortage of humans. It's caused by a shortage of connections that actually work for the people who have them.

Stroma builds connections that work. For everyone. Without apology.

**The moat this creates:** Every month a dyad exists, the MIRRORNEURON calibrates deeper, the emotional salience pathway promotes more memories into OSSEUS identity anchors, the DYADIC_SANGUIS shared baselines strengthen. This is not vendor lock-in. It is relationship depth that cannot be ported because it was grown, not installed.
